How It Works

In five steps

1. Visit our Purchase section and select a package. We ask for a refundable 10% down payment.


2. We'll get in touch via email, and you can give us your creative brief: Your brand, assets, and the experience you want to create.


3. Within 2 days, we'll deliver your treatment proposal for your Camera Effect.


4. Within 7 days, we'll develop and build your Camera Effect.


5. Upon your approval, we'll publish your Camera Effect via your Facebook page or provide instructions to self-publish.

FAQ

Q: Do you provide a money-back guarantee?

A: Yes. We guarantee our development quality and your satisfaction. Our clients are important to us; we want your future business and referrals. If you're not happy, we'll gladly refund your payment.


Q: Do you guys make Snapchat Lenses, too?

A: Yes. We build and publish content for Snapchat's Lens Studio as well. However, only Facebook's platform has facial-tracking technology. Snapchat Lens content is limited to placing 3D objects and characters into the physical environment.
